Hello, my name is Ian and I'm a researcher at the Georgia Tech Research Institute here in Atlanta. Today I'm going to talk to you a little bit about our journey of building an AI application. I hope that by sharing some of our experiences, both the good and the bad, that we can help you accelerate your journey on building your own AI applications. For the last two years, I've been working on a project with the goal of detecting anomalous behavior and commercial fishing vessels. When we started, AI was a very small component of what we were considering doing. However, as the project progressed, it became increasingly obvious that AI was going to play a central role in what we needed to do. When we made that realization, we started looking for tools that would help us build an AI application faster. What we found was a myriad of tools designed to solve individual problems related to building AI applications, but nothing that provided an end-to-end framework, from ingest all the way through to deployment, to help us build a complete AI application.
